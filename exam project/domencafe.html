History

The dome is a landmark on Aarhus Harbor and a symbol of, among other things, innovation,
citizenship and green transformation in Aarhus Municipality.
Domen is a city life house with a view to the sky and sea. It is located at the far end of Pier 2 in
the temporary city park. In 2018, Innovation and Citizenship took over the Dome, on behalf of
Aarhus Municipality, in order to provide an informal, inspiring and sustainable meeting place
for both citizens, associations and businesses.
All initiatives and activities held in Domen support citizenship, sustainability and community.
The dome's 450 square meters, spread over two levels, provides space for both a climate café
and bar, office spaces, a stage and a conference hall, which can accommodate around 150
people, and a lot of green plants that thrive in the fantastic light, which together create a
whole special atmosphere.

Domen's plants

Domen is not only a cozy café and a place for citizenship with exciting cultural events and
arrangements. It is also a green greenhouse.
Here you will find a myriad of different plants, all of which require care and attention in order to
thrive and create an atmosphere for the guests in the Dome.

The Cafe

During Domen's opening hours, you can also visit the café and let yourself be tempted by the
many different offers. The court does not have food and hot dishes. But serve pastries and
bread. The café and bar is non-profit and is run jointly by the municipality, citizens and associations as
part of an employment effort, which we call city life jobs. Therefore, it is also not permitted to
bring your own food and drink into the Dome. Follow Domen's Facebook page, which is continuously updated with news, events and
opening hours.
