Discover the hidden beauty of Aarhus

Marselisborg Deer Park

To the south of Aarhus city, within the Thors
woods—a component of the expansive
Marselisborg-Moesgaard forest—sits the Deer
Park. Within this area, a delightful population
of sika and fallow deer awaits, offering visitors
the opportunity for up-close encounters. The
wild boars live in their own enclosure.

The Tropical Houses

The Tropical Houses, known as
"Væksthusene" in Danish, located within
the Botanical Garden in Aarhus, are a
place where numerous plant collections
are presented and preserved in four
different climate zones.

Domen Cafe

Experience the magic of Domen Cafe,
under a beautiful glass dome. Immerse
yourself in the lush greenery indoors
surrounded by thriving plants, while
outside, a breathtaking garden and
lively wildlife create a perfect harmony.
