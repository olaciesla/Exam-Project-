About

Botanisk Have has a perfect location right in the middle of Aarhus, so it's easy to get here, no
matter where you are in the city. It is therefore not far from the city's exciting museums, the
popular cafés along the Åboulevard and abundant shops in the Latin Quarter or Bruun's
Gallery - depending on what you are in the mood for.

History

The park was originally created primarily as a botanical and academic study-ground in relation
to Aarhus University and provides an attraction of interest to those who wish to inspect the
thousands of plant species on display here. The landscape is quite varied and includes a large
greenhouse center; originally from 1970, but with a newly added tropical hothouse.
The greenhouses has been heavily renovated from 2011 to late 2014 and a large tropical dome
was added, amongst other facilities.

An educational and sensuous experience

Explore the Tropical Houses at the Botanical Garden for an educational and sensory journey.
Immerse yourself in a rich learning experience as you discover diverse plant collections thriving
in four distinct climate zones. The vibrant colors, lush aromas, and the soothing atmosphere
create a truly immersive encounter, making it an educational and sensuous adventure for all
visitors.

Fascinating stories about nature and botany

Listen to fascinating stories about nature and botany while discovering the connection
between humans and the natural world. Explore the outer areas of the Botanical Gardens to
experience classic Danish landscapes like heath and beach, inspiring you to explore the real
nature on the outskirts of Aarhus. Have a picnic, enjoy your time alone or with friends!

Enjoy a delicious lunch in the café

Discover the Tropical Houses Café nestled within the Botanical Gardens—an oasis in the city
center. Treat yourself to a delightful light lunch or indulge in scrumptious cakes in this serene
haven.
